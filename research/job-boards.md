# Job Boards & Platforms

## Remote-First

- [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/) — largest remote work community
- [Remote.co](https://remote.co/remote-jobs/) — curated remote jobs
- [Remotive](https://remotive.com/) — remote tech jobs
- [Working Nomads](https://workingnomads.co/) — curated remote listings
- [Himalayas](https://himalayas.app/) — remote jobs with company profiles
- [Arc.dev](https://arc.dev/) — vetted remote developers
- [Remote100K](https://remote100k.io/) — remote jobs paying $100K+

## Startup / Founder-Backed

- [Wellfound (AngelList)](https://wellfound.com/) — startup jobs with salary transparency
- [Y Combinator Work at a Startup](https://www.ycombinator.com/jobs) — YC portfolio companies
- [Startup.jobs](https://startup.jobs/) — startup job aggregator

## Community / Curated

- [HN Who is Hiring](https://hnhiring.com/) — monthly Hacker News thread (filter by "remote")
- [whoishiring.work](https://whoishiring.work/) — HN thread aggregator
- [Key Values](https://www.keyvalues.com/) — find teams by engineering culture

## AI / ML Specific

- [AI Jobs](https://aijobs.net/) — AI and ML job board

- [PromptlyHired](https://promptlyhired.com/) — AI/LLM job board

- [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/) — salary data and job listings
